Route Planner

Plot a route anywhere.

Drag/zoom the map to find where you would like your route.

Click to add points and see your route grow.

If you click the hand icon at the top of the map you can edit the line. Drag any of the points to move it. Double click a point to remove it. Dragging the paler circle in the middle of a line segment allows you to add extra points within the line. You can use this to start with a rough route and add more detail later.

You can click the Mode button to switch between Edit and View modes. View Mode hides the route markers and control points and prevents accidental edits.
